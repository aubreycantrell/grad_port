Work & Projects

About

I’m a senior at Wellesley College, double majoring in Computer Science and Studio Art, passionate about the intersection of technology, design, and learning. My work lies in the realm of designing developmental tools, and researching how AI can be implemented to support creativity and learning.

While the creativity is always flowing, when I’m not designing, you can find me in the print studio, mountains, or ocean!

Felt Tide: Investigations and Lessons of the Pacific Surf — a year-long exploration through printmaking, welding, fiber arts, and digital media inspired by my experience surfing in the Pacific Northwest.

Advisor: Prof. Phyllis McGibbon

Ongoing investigations include the depiction of time, iteration, immersion, and movement in static imagery.

Research focus: the context of water, art’s ability to “show not tell,” and how viewers interpret immersive experiences.
